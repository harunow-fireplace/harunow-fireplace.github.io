Harunow Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

At Harunow, we are deeply committed to protecting children and ensuring our platform remains a safe and respectful space for all users. We have established the following standards to address and respond to any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE):

Our Commitment

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Harunow has a strict zero-tolerance policy for any content or behavior that involves the abuse, exploitation, or endangerment of children. Violations will result in immediate account termination and other necessary actions to ensure the safety of our platform.

2. User-Driven Reporting

Harunow relies on its community to report any suspicious or harmful activity. We provide accessible reporting tools within the platform, enabling users to flag inappropriate content or behavior for review by our moderation team.

3. Cooperation with Authorities

While Harunow does not independently detect CSAE content, we take all reported cases seriously. We investigate promptly and cooperate fully with law enforcement and relevant authorities to ensure that perpetrators are held accountable.

4. Safety by Design

Harunow integrates features designed to promote a safer online environment. These include:

  • Moderation of reported content by trained team members.
  • Age-appropriate features to protect younger users.
  • Tools for parents and guardians to monitor and guide platform use.

5. Educational Initiatives

We are committed to raising awareness among our users about online safety, including how to recognize, report, and avoid harmful content or interactions.

How We Handle Reports

  • User Reports: Users can report inappropriate content or behavior by contacting us support@harunow.com
  • Moderation Review: A dedicated team reviews each report carefully and takes swift action, which may include removing content, banning accounts, and escalating the issue to law enforcement.
  • Cooperation: Harunow fully supports investigations by providing relevant information and assistance to authorities when required.

Contact Us

If you encounter any content or activity on Harunow that may involve CSAE, please report it using our in-app tools or contact us at support@harunow.com. Together, we can create a safer online environment for everyone.
