Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28/1/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Harunow collects, uses, and protects user data.

1. Data We Collect

For authentication purposes, we use AWS Cognito, which stores your name and email address.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate users and provide access to Harunow.
  • We do not use data for marketing or tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Harunow:

  • AWS Amplify, Cognito, S3, API Gateway, Lambda, Firebase
These services help with authentication, storage, and backend functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data.
  • Modify their account information.
  • Request deletion of their account and data.
To exercise these rights, users can contact us at hello@chillpod.studio.

6. Security Measures

We take security seriously and implement:

  • AWS Cognito for secure authentication.
  • Data encryption (SSL/TLS for transmission, S3 encryption for storage).
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to restrict unauthorized access.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Harunow does not use traditional cookies or tracking mechanisms for analytics or advertising. However, we use AWS Amplify and Cognito, which store authentication tokens to maintain user sessions. These tokens are necessary for login functionality and do not track user activity beyond authentication.

8. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in Malaysia or other countries where our servers or service providers are located. By using Harunow, you consent to such transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated version will be effective as of the date indicated at the top of the page.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Malaysia.
